Spaghetti Italiana

Ingredients

  • 1 package spaghetti
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 3 tablespoon canola oil
  • 1 jar meatless spaghetti sauce
  • 1 can garbanzo beans
  • 1 can diced tomatoes with garlic and onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
Add to shopping list >

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package directions. Meanwhile, in a larger skillet, saute the onion, celery, and garlic powder in oil until tender. Add the spaghetti sauce, beans, tomatoes, sugar, salt, oregano and bay leaf.
  2. Bring to boil and simmer for 10 minutes. Discard bay leaf. Drain spaghetti; top with sauce and cheese.
